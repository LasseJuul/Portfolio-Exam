Prototype

The prototype project was a group project focused on researching and developing a prototype of what a website would look like. It had a very specific sender and target audience in mind with the Danish government and international students.


I had the resonsibility of researching accommodation websites and building the prototype in Adobe XD from sketches and inserting the content, aswell as making a style tile and conducting the "Think aloud test". The experience of working in XD was really rewarding, expecially when going from a low-fidelity prototype to a high-fidelity and seeing it take more and more shape.

What i've learned

Mostly what i took with me from this project was skills in XD. The software is extremely usefull to help visuallize and prepare before coding, and by making a low-fidelity. Also it taught me to always keep in mind where to place content regarding the purpose of the website. By having a mental map you make a superior user expereince and help users to easily navigate the site while keeping it pleasing to the eye. This also comes down to visual and navigational design conventions.

I also got to improve on previous skills such as making a style tile and the "Think aloud test", which was similar to interviewing reserach in some ways. Having in mind that the questions shold be very precise for optimal outcome.
From previous style tiles i added some of the navigational tools from the UI kit which could later be added to the prototype.
